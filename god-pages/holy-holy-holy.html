Holy Holy Holy!

“Holy, holy, holy is the Lord Almighty;
the whole earth is full of his glory.” — Revelation 4:8 (NIV)

Holy Hearts of One

Holy, holy, holy—
One heart, one pulse, one flame,
A single light that calls all names.

Holy, holy, holy —
Consciousness, a boundless sea,
Flowing through each mystery.

Many voices, one accord,
Manifold in one, the sacred Lord.

Holy, holy, holy—
The manifold in unity,
Each facet shining endlessly.

One essence weaving every part,
A holy thrice-repeated heart.

Holy, holy, holy—
Infinite in sacred form,
The source where all true selves are born.

“Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty,
who was, and is, and is to come.” — Revelation 4:8 (NIV)
